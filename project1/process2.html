Step 1: pickling. Chicken wings need to absorb seasonings


Step 2: Heat the pan and pour oil,
then add the spices and marinated chicken wings and fry until the surface of the wings turns a golden yellow


Step 3: Pour in cola and a small amount of seasoning,
stir fry, cover with a lid, and simmer over low heat


Step 4: After simmering for a period of time, open the lid of the pot,
flip the chicken wings, and let them taste evenly


Step 5: Make sure to keep only a small amount of sauce in the pot
and sprinkle the sauce on top of the chicken wings on a plate


The above steps are the simplest version of the recipe,
and if you want to make it more successful and delicious, there are many things to pay attention to
